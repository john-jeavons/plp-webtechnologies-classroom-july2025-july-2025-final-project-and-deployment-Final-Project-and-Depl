About Us

Learn about our history, values, and educational philosophy

Our History

Lenana Girls High School was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education to young women in Kenya. Starting with just 50 students and 5 teachers, the school has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the country.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities to include modern classrooms, science laboratories, a library, computer labs, and sports facilities. Our commitment to excellence has remained unwavering throughout our journey.

Educational Philosophy

At Lenana Girls High School, we believe in providing a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, physical, social, and emotional development of our students. Our approach to education is student-centered, focusing on individual strengths and areas for growth.

We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication skills, preparing our students for the challenges of the 21st century. Our curriculum is designed to foster a love for learning and a spirit of inquiry.
